Game Instructions
This Simon game flashes one of the four coloured buttons as you progress through the levels, and at each level you have to remember the buttons pressed in previous levels, followed by the currently flashed button. For eg- Level 1- Red btn flashes. You press it and move to Level 2. Just then, another one flashes, say Blue. So you press Red, then Blue. Then comes Level 3, another flash say blue, again. You press Red, Blue, Blue. This is the way you progress. There's one check keeping you from pressing the buttons too quickly. You do not have to press the same button again if it still has the TARDIS flashing inside it. (the TARDIS is the blue box that flashes when you click on a button while the game is running) So, wait for the TARDIS to disappear, then press the button.

Press any Key on your keyboard to Start
